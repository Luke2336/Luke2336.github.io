  • Instantaneous Power $$\displaystyle\begin{aligned} i(t) &= I_m \cos (\omega t + \theta_i)\\ v(t) &= V_m \cos (\omega t + \theta_v)\\ p(t) &= \frac{1}{2} I_m V_m (\cos(\theta_v - \theta_i) + \cos(2\omega t + \theta_v + \theta_i)) \end{aligned}$$

  • Average Power $$\displaystyle\begin{aligned} P &= \frac{1}{T} \int_0^T p(t) dt \\ &= \frac{1}{2} I_m V_m \cos(\theta_v - \theta_i) \\ &= \frac{1}{2} Re[\mathbb{VI}^*] \end{aligned}$$

  • Maximum Average Power Transform $$\displaystyle\begin{aligned} \mathbb{Z}_{Th} &= R_{th} + j X_{Th},\mathbb{Z}_{L} = R_{L} + j X_{L} \\ \mathbb{I} &= \frac{ \mathbb{V}_{Th} }{\mathbb{Z}_{Th} + \mathbb{Z}_{L}} = \frac{ \mathbb{V}_{Th} }{(R_{th} + R_L) + j (X_{Th} + X_L)} \\ P &= \frac{1}{2} Re[\mathbb{VI} ^ *] = \frac{1}{2} Re[\mathbb{Z}_{L} \mathbb{II} ^ *] = \frac{1}{2} |\mathbb{I}| ^ 2 {R}_{L} \\ &= \frac{ | \mathbb{V}_{Th} | ^ 2 {R}_{L} }{({R}_{th} + {R}_{L}) ^ 2 + ({X}_{Th} + {X}_{L}) ^ 2} \\ \frac{\partial P}{\partial X _L} &= 0 \implies X _ L = - X _ {Th} \\ \frac{\partial P}{\partial R _L} &= 0 \implies R _ L = R _ {Th} \\ \implies \mathbb{Z}_{L} &= \mathbb{Z}_{Th} ^ * \implies P_{max} = \frac{|\mathbb{V}_{Th}|^2}{8} \end{aligned}$$

  • Effective Value / RMS Value

$$\begin{aligned} P_R &= \frac{1}{T} \int_0^T i^2 R dt = I_{eff}^2 R \\ I_{eff} &= \sqrt{\frac{1}{T} \int_0^T i^2 dt} = I_{rms} \\ I_{rms} &= \sqrt{\frac{1}{T} \int_0^T I_m^2 \cos^2 (\omega t + \theta_i)dt} \\ &= \sqrt{\frac{1}{T} \int_0^T I_m^2 (1 + \cos (2\omega t + 2\theta_i))dt} \\ &= \frac{I_m}{\sqrt 2} \\ P &= I_{rms}^2 R = \frac{V_{rms}^2}{R} \end{aligned}$$
